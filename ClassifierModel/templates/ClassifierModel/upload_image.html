About System

This tool designed to inspect and classify a manufactured product into one of three categories: "Good," "Flawed," or "Broken."

It takes Images of newly manufactured products as input and outputs a prediction of the product's condition. It is based on Deep Learning and Computer Vision.

Features

- Real-time Decision Making by providing the ability to insert an image and get the real time inference result.
- Highly accurate model in the identification of product conditions
- The model is trained on over 1000 real product images
- Easy and Simple to use Web Interface
- Modle is ready to be integrated in a production line.

In a production lines,

The system inspects products as they move along a conveyor belt.

A camera captures images of these products, which are then analyzed by the machine learning model,
to determine their condition.

- If a product is in good condition, it proceeds along the conveyor belt.

- If a product exhibits flaws, it is categorized as "Flawed" and removed from the production line.

- If a product is severely damaged, it is marked as "Broken" and removed from the production line.

- Products without any defects are classified as "Good" and are prepared for shipment to customers.

In This Demonstration:

The system provides real-time inference through a web application developed in Django.

The web app is deployed on Azure for accessibility.

Try the System

  • Click the "Choose File" button to upload an image of the product for analysis.
  • After selecting the image, click "Upload and Predict."
  • The system utilizes a pre-trained CNN model for multiclass classification based on the image.
  • The output will be displayed, indicating whether the product is "Good," "Flawed," or "Broken."
  • Users can also access prediction probabilities to gauge the model's confidence in its assessment.
